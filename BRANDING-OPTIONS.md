# Vizibly Branding - 3 Krachtige Richtingen

## Option 1: BRUTALIST BLACK ‚ö´
**Tagline:** "Raw power. Zero fluff."

### Kleurenpalet
- **Primary:** Pure black (#000000) met stark white (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent:** Electric lime green (#CCFF00) voor CTAs
- **Secondary:** Dark grays (#1A1A1A, #2D2D2D)
- **Highlights:** Neon green borders/underlines

### Typography
- **Headings:** **Space Grotesk** (ultra-bold, 800 weight)
  - Tight leading (-0.05em letter-spacing)
  - MASSIVE sizes (80px+ for h1)
- **Body:** **Inter** (regular, 400-500)
  - Readable, clean, technical

### Visual Style
- **Harsh borders** - thick (4-6px), black outlines
- **No roundings** - sharp 90¬∞ corners everywhere
- **Grid-based layouts** - strict alignment
- **High contrast** - pure black on white
- **Bold typography** - huge, impactful headlines
- **Minimal imagery** - focus on typography and data
- **ASCII-art style decorations**
- **Terminal/command-line aesthetics**

### Components
- Cards with thick black borders, no shadows
- Buttons: solid black with lime green hover
- Stats: Large numbers in black, lime accents
- Navigation: Fixed header, black background, white text

### Vibe
Unapologetic. Direct. No BS. Like a punk rock poster meets terminal UI.

## Option 3: BOLD INDUSTRIAL üî∂
**Tagline:** "Built to rank."

### Kleurenpalet
- **Primary:** Charcoal black (#1C1C1C)
- **Accent:** Safety orange (#FF6B00)
- **Secondary:** Steel gray (#4A5568)
- **Backgrounds:** Off-white (#F8F8F8) and dark (#252525)
- **Highlights:** Bright orange warnings/CTAs

### Typography
- **Headings:** **Archivo Black** or **Druk Wide**
  - Ultra-bold, condensed, powerful
  - All-caps for impact sections
- **Body:** **Work Sans** (clean, readable)

### Visual Style
- **Industrial textures** - subtle noise/grain
- **Angular shapes** - diagonal cuts, bold geometry
- **Orange safety accents** - warning tape aesthetic
- **Thick dividers** - bold orange lines between sections
- **Photo overlays** - duotone with orange tint
- **Data-viz style** - charts, numbers, metrics everywhere
- **Concrete/metal feel** - rough textures

### Components
- Cards: White with orange border-left (8px thick)
- Buttons: Solid orange, black text, sharp corners
- Stats: Huge numbers with orange highlights
- Navigation: Black header with orange accent stripe

### Vibe
Construction site meets data lab. Raw, powerful, results-driven.

## RECOMMENDATION

**Voor Vizibly zou ik kiezen:** **Option 1 (Brutalist Black)**

**Waarom:**
- SEO is data-driven - brutalism communiceert dat perfect
- Onderscheidend in een zee van gradient-happy competitors
- Geen-nonsense approach past bij "resultaten boven praatjes"
- Bold typography = bold claims (je moet backuppen met resultaten)
- Easy to maintain, scales well
- Timeless (brutalism isn't a passing trend)

**Second choice:** Option 3 (Industrial) - as it shares the bold, data-driven vibe but with more warmth via orange.
